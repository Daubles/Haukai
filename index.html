Haukai Restaurant

About Us

Nestled in the heart of the Bay of Islands, Haukai Restaurant has proudly been locally owned and operated by husband and wife, John and Kath, for 28 years.

With a focus on Māori cuisine and culture, Haukai creates an exciting, quintessential New Zealand dining experience, that has been enjoyed by locals and tourists from around the world.

All of our ingredients are sourced locally and sustainably to ensure we're doing our bit to keep Aotearoa looking beautiful.
